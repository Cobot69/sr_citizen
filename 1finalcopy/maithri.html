About Us

  • Maithri Vrudashrama is a free old age home located in Bairidevarakoppa, Hubballi.
  • It is a peaceful and welcoming place designed to provide care and support for elderly residents.
  • Currently, the ashram houses 25 senior citizens, with 5 dedicated staff members who ensure a friendly and homely atmosphere.
  • The staff are known for their caring nature, making every resident feel valued and comfortable.
  • The ashram provides all the basic necessities, including regular medical checkups, nutritious meals, and a clean, well-maintained living environment.
  • Residents engage in extra activities like bhajans, which foster a sense of community and spiritual well-being.
  • These activities help the elderly stay active and involved, adding joy and meaning to their daily lives.
  • Situated far from the main road, Maithri Vrudashrama offers a serene environment with minimal noise pollution.
  • The peaceful surroundings and fresh air make it an ideal place for senior citizens to live in comfort.
  • The ashram accepts both male and female residents, ensuring separate accommodations to maintain privacy.
  • With its friendly staff, supportive environment, and a range of activities, Maithri Vrudashrama truly feels like home for its residents.
  • It is a place where elderly individuals can live with dignity and peace, making it a reliable and compassionate choice for senior care in Hubballi.
