About Us

  • Jeevan Sandhya concept was the dream project of Vidyaranya Seva Sangha, a nonprofit social organization.
  • Vidyaranya Seva Sangha is managed by doctors, engineers, and businessmen, focused on reaching the unreached.
  • Registered in 1984, the organization is involved in many causes and aims to create a movement in society to form a broad-based, strong, and caring institution.
  • The organization emphasizes maintaining smiling faces and dignity of residents.
  • Jeevan Sandhya offers independent, semi, and multi-dwellings designed with all modern amenities in a serene atmosphere.
  • The facility is slightly off the main Karwar Road amidst greenery, providing a peaceful environment.
  • Supported by various institutions, Jeevan Sandhya celebrates many events, including entertainment like drama, pravachanas, sangeet, and student performances.
  • These events make life more enjoyable for its residents, fostering a lively and engaging community.
